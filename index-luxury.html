Our Story

Where Connection Meets Nature

During years of travel, we kept meeting people who wanted more than a desk with a view. They wanted a place that held both personal space and meaningful connection without forcing either.

Sonrisa was created as a calmer answer: private rooms, restorative routines, and a community culture that feels warm rather than loud. Madeira gave it the right setting. The intention shaped everything else.

A sanctuary for people from all over the world to arrive, soften, and find peace.

Founded by two brothers

Building a slower, more human co-living experience since 2024.
