## 📋 Deploy in 3 Steps (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Netlify
Open https://app.netlify.com in your browser

### Step 2: Sign Up (Free)
- Use GitHub, GitLab, or Email
- Free plan is perfect for this site

### Step 3: Deploy
- Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
- Drag the **entire folder** `sonrisa-coliving` into the upload zone
- Wait 30 seconds
- Done! ✨

**Your site will be live at:** `https://[random-name].netlify.app`

### Photos to Replace
Current site uses beautiful Unsplash placeholder images. Replace with:

1. **Hero Background** - Paul do Mar landscape
2. **About Section** - Team/guests interacting
3. **Rooms Page** - 3 photos per room × 8 rooms = 24 photos minimum
4. **Co-working Space** - Workspace photo
5. **Kitchen** - Communal kitchen
6. **Garden** - Outdoor areas

**How to update:**
1. Put photos in `images/` folder
2. Edit `js/rooms-data.js` for room photos
3. Edit `js/sections.js` for main page
4. Replace Unsplash URLs with `images/your-photo.jpg`
5. Re-deploy (drag folder to Netlify again)
